Amin Al Hazwani

I lead Design Operations at Accurat, a data-driven research, design, and innovation studio based in Milan, Italy.

Until 2018, I lived in Berlin and worked for Mozilla. I designed web experiences for TVs and smart assistants powered by Amazon’s Alexa. I also contributed to the launch of the Firefox Design System.

In 2016, I taught Web and Media Design at the M.A. in Eco-Social Design in Bolzano, Italy. I co-wrote a piece for A List Apart.

Between 2009–16 I ran a one-man studio, specialized in design for screens. I produced work for Google, Studio Mut, Arillo, Unibz and more.
